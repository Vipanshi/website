Future Jobs

  • Chartered Accountant

    • A Chartered Accountant (CA) is responsible for the financial efficiency of a company or their client

  • Company Secretary

    • A CS acts as a link between the company and other concerned parties.

  • Banking

    • Banking sector holds a lot of lucrative job opportunities for the commerce students. It is among the most hardcore commerce fields

  • Tax Consultant

    • Tax consultants or tax advisers are the individuals who have expertise in tax law. A commerce graduate with necessary skills and tax knowledge can either work with a firm or can work as an individual.

  • Cost & work Accountant

    • These professionals deal with budgeting, cost & asset management, and performance evaluation within a company or an organisation.

  • Actuary

    • An actuary is like a future predictor of a company or an organisation.They deal with making the balance between the design and price of the product. They predict & evaluate the future events and their impact on the financial status of an organisation.
